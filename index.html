Our Story

The Science of Delicious

Named after the Maillard reaction‚Äîthe chemical process that creates that irresistible crust on our smashed patties‚ÄîMaillards was born from a passion for the perfect burger.

Founded by Max Ponzurick, a former Virginia Tech baseball player who discovered his true calling in the kitchen, we've been serving up SF's favorite smash burgers at the Outer Sunset Farmers Market since day one.

Every burger features paper-thin patties with a seductive, delicate crunch, topped with our signature caramelized shallots, house-made pickles, and that legendary Maillard sauce.

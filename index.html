Our Focus

The Burger

Maillard /maɪˈjɑːr/ — named for the reaction, the browning that makes a smash burger worth chasing.

Hard-smashed on a hot flat-top for deep browning and lacy edges. Built to stay simple: beef, cheese, pickles, shallots, sauce, bun.
